What will I learn in the type 2 course?

  • Why is blood sugar important?

    Groceries, exercise, stress, almost everything affects you and your blood sugar. We’ll show you step by step how you avoid sugar traps and how to measure correctly.

  • What can I eat?

    Anything! But smartly and in moderation. With some simple tips & tricks we’ll show you how you still can enjoy food (even your favorite dishes). You’ll be eating healthier in no time.

  • Do I have to exercise?

    Yes, but we promise – no marathons, triathlons, or gym memberships required. We’ll show you how to build exercise into your daily life and stay motivated. No sweat! ...Ok, maybe a little.
